Project Overview

The IoTcloudServe@TEIN project represents a groundbreaking initiative to design, develop, and operate a centralized IoT-cloud service platform in Thailand, accessible to TEIN communities worldwide.


This innovative platform demonstrates its utility through three data-centric IoT-cloud services across two strategic TEIN sites: Smart-Energy/Mobility@Chula in Thailand and Smart-Agriculture@NUOL in Laos.


The project integrates collaborative efforts from developed, developing, and least developed countries to foster IoT-domain service diversification through shared resources, data, and learned lessons.

Project Activities

🤝 Collaborative Learning

Three collaboration meetings held at APAN46 (New Zealand), APAN47 (Korea), and virtual sessions. Two IoTcloudServe@TEIN workshops conducted in Laos with comprehensive training programs.

🏗️ Infrastructure Development

Complete hardware procurement and installation of main server towers (Data Lake) at ThaiREN in Bangkok, Thailand, serving as central computing and storage resources.

💻 Platform Engineering

Comprehensive service platform design and development, hosted within the Data Lake, leveraging NETPIE and KAFKA frameworks for optimal performance.

🚀 Live Demonstrations

Implementation of Smart-Energy/Mobility@Chula (Thailand) and Smart-Agriculture@NUOL (Laos) demonstration sites showcasing real-world applications.
